Crockpot Garlic Chicken Parm

Description

This easy-to-make pasta dish can feed you for days, and is a delicious crockpot dish that you can start in the morning and come back to for dinner later.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 1/2 tsp each of salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp each of paprika, onion powder, and Italian seasoning
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • Buffalo Wild Wing's Garlic Parmesan sauce
  • 1 jar of Alfredo sauce
  • 1 16 oz box of pasta
  • 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. Add the chicken, salt, pepper, paprika, and Italian seasoning to the slow cooker.
  2. Add minced garlic, and enough garlic parmesan sauce to cover the chicken fully.
  3. Cook on low heat for 4 to 6 hours.
  4. When fully cooked, shred or cube chicken and return to slow cooker.
  5. Cook the pasta, following instructions on the pasta packaging.
  6. Add Alfredo sauce, parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, to the slow cooker. Stir together well.
  7. When fininshed cooking, add the cooked pasta to the slow cooker and mix everything together.
  8. Serve.
